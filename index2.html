White Birch Resort

on Blackduck Lake

5-Star All-Inclusive Lakefront Luxury Resort

for all occasions year-round

White Birch Resort offers luxury vacation homes and cabin accommodations overlooking Blackduck Lake and the “lazy” knee-deep Blackduck River, surrounded by awe-inspiring panoramic views of deep green and blue landscapes. Experience the unspoiled natural beauty of birch, spruce, maples, vibrant sunsets and calm summer breezes.

Originally established in 1932, this beautiful 5-star lakefront resort is proud to offer an amazing experience like no other resort in Minnesota. Many guests say they feel as though they are in their own private custom lake home!

Enjoy fishing, kayaking, canoeing, boating and swimming in the summer, and ice fishing, snowmobiling, snowshoeing and cross-country skiing in the winter. More than 30 all-inclusive on-resort experiences and activities are offered for guests to enjoy.

The area around White Birch Resort offers over 90 local attractions and activities including golfing, hiking, biking, skiing, snow tubing, sightseeing, shopping and much more. Conveniently located just minutes north of Bemidji, 3½ hours north of Minneapolis, 3 hours east of Fargo and 1 hour south of Canada, White Birch Resort is close to the surrounding communities of Blackduck, Bemidji, Upper Red Lake, Park Rapids, Grand Rapids and Walker.

Come experience everything White Birch Resort has to offer:
  • Unmatched 5-star luxury lake homes and cabins that can accommodate up to 22 people
  • Only resort offering private hot tubs at each home
  • Located on a "Top 10 Best" walleye fishing lake
  • Over 30 on-resort activities included FREE
  • Warm personalized service on site 24/7
  • Best rates for 5-star small resort accommodations and activities
  • Open year-round
