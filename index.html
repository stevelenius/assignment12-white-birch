5-STAR All-Inclusive Lakefront Luxury Resort for all occasions Year-Round White Birch Resort is surrounded by awe-inspiring panoramic views of deep greens and blue landscapes, and the unspoiled natural beauty of birch, spruce, maples, vibrant sunsets and calm summer breezes. Originally established in 1932, this beautiful 5-star lakefront resort is proud to offer an amazing experience like no other resort in Minnesota. This BRAND NEW 5-STAR ALL-INCLUSIVE resort in Minnesota now offers luxury vacation homes and cabin accommodations overlooking Blackduck Lake and the “lazy” knee deep Blackduck River, just minutes north of Bemidji, 3 ½ hours north of Minneapolis, 3 hours east of Fargo and 1 hour south of Canada. The White Birch Resort experience is for those individuals who seek and appreciate a well-deserved taste of luxury resorts while on vacation. With a relaxed approach to luxury for groups of all ages and sizes, guests are excited and guaranteed to enjoy 2600 acres of four season's recreational adventure to explore, accompanied with endless and unmatched resort amenities minus the crowds. Many guests say they feel as though they are in their own private custom lake home. The resort has more than 30 ALL-INCLUSIVE on-resort experiences and activities for guests to enjoy. Guests can take advantage of all forms of lake recreation, such as, fishing kayaking, canoeing, boating and swimming in the summer to ice fishing, snowmobiling, snow shoeing and cross-country skiing in the winter. Even though White Birch Resort has ample amounts of on-resort entertainment, the area also features access to over 90 local attractions and activities including golfing, hiking, biking, skiing, snow tubing, sightseeing, shopping and much more. White Birch Resort is close to the surrounding communities of Blackduck, Bemidji, Upper Red Lake, Park Rapids, Grand Rapids and Walker. Once you've stayed at White Birch Resort, it is easy to see why this picturesque destination has always been proud to lure guests to a multi-generational tradition as demonstrated by repeat guests. Guests no longer come to just fulfill dreams of fishing at an iconic "Premier Top 10 BEST" fishing camps. WBR is a northern slice of paradise for vacationers to enjoy! So, if you are looking to unleash your inner child, take part in a retreat or simply just sit around the fire to relax, whatever your reason for choosing White Birch Resort, Minnesota luxury resorts can virtually cater to endless year round recreational opportunities to entertain everyone in your group, while providing fabulous unmatched accommodations at incredible rates. Come experience everything White Birch Resort has to offer.
